Prevention of water pollution

There are a number of things that can be done to prevent and reduce water pollution. These include:
Treating sewage and wastewater properly: This involves removing harmful bacteria, viruses, and parasites. Reducing the use of pesticides and herbicides: This can help to reduce the amount of these chemicals that run off into water bodies. Managing stormwater runoff: This can help to prevent sediment and other pollutants from entering water bodies. Cleaning up oil spills and other leaks: This helps to prevent these pollutants from spreading and harming aquatic life.
