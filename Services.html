Causes of water pollution

Water pollution stems from industrial discharges, agricultural runoff (fertilizers, pesticides), urbanization (stormwater runoff), improper wastewater disposal, oil spills, mining activities, deforestation, and atmospheric deposition. These anthropogenic activities introduce harmful substances, compromising water quality and necessitating concerted efforts for effective management and prevention.
